How to export your history

💬 ChatGPT

  1. Settings → Data Controls
  2. Click "Export data"
  3. Wait for email from OpenAI
  4. Upload conversations.json

🤖 Claude

  1. Settings → Privacy
  2. Click "Export conversations"
  3. Download the JSON file
  4. Upload it here

✨ Gemini

  1. Go to takeout.google.com
  2. Select "Gemini Apps Activity"
  3. Export and download
  4. Upload the JSON file

𝕏 Grok

  1. Go to x.com → Settings
  2. Privacy → Your Account
  3. Click "Request your archive"
  4. Upload grok-conversations.json
