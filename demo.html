Cognitive Summary
Balance is the rarest cognitive signature — and this profile has it at depth. At 45 words per message across 214 conversations over 26 months, Isla does not optimise for any single mode of thinking. She does not push back often (9%), but when she does, the question is surgical. She asks more than she declares (22% question rate), but the questions have architecture. What emerges across two years of longitudinal data is the portrait of a mind that connects things others keep separate — operating with what cognitive scientists call remote associative capacity, moving between domains without losing the thread that runs through all of them.
